# Introduction
 
Welcome to MyMusic REST API documentation. From here you can easily discover all available REST APIs with the required information on how to consume it.

## What is MyMusic?

![Image External](../public/demo.png)

With MyMusic, it&apos;s easy to find the right music for every moment - on your phone, your computer, your tablet and more.
